About YLASS

Building the Next Generation of South Sudanese Leaders

Our Story

The Young Leaders Academy of South Sudan (YLASS) was founded by Emmanuel Malou Deng and Piok Deng Mayiik, South Sudanese students at Columbia University and Dartmouth College respectively. The program was established after the realization that academically talented students from South Sudan were missing out on admissions to top universities due to the daunting U.S. college application process.

YLASS provides students with the resources and knowledge to overcome the bottlenecks that prevent deserving students from accessing an American education. Our students apply directly to universities, and it is the universities that award them scholarships. YLASS prepares students to become so competitive that rejecting them over other applicants is almost impossible.

Our Mission

Global Competitiveness

Prepare South Sudanese students to compete globally with comprehensive college preparatory support.

Full Scholarships

Increase the number of South Sudanese students admitted to top U.S. universities with full scholarships.

Nation Building

Build a generation of well-educated leaders who will contribute to South Sudan's development in medicine, engineering, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

Our Program

A 12-Month Comprehensive College Prep Journey

Our rigorous program equips students with every tool they need to successfully navigate the U.S. college application process and thrive at elite institutions.

Critical Thinking

Assigned reading comprehension, academic seminars, and mathematics to sharpen analytical skills.

Academic Writing

Building the writing skills necessary for success at college, from essays to research papers.

Test Preparation

Comprehensive preparation for standardized tests including the SAT, ACT, and TOEFL.

Personal Essay Coaching

One-on-one sessions to identify and craft compelling personal narratives from each student's unique lived experience.

Mentorship

Guidance from current university students and alumni who have walked the same path.

Leadership & Career

Leadership development, career guidance workshops, and university readiness courses to build proud, patriotic citizens ready to contribute to nation-building.
