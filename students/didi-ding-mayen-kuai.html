Didi Ding Mayen Kuai

Juba Academy, Juba

Didi was a bright and ambitious member of YLASS's founding cohort who earned admission to Babson College on a full scholarship. She passed away in March 2024 and is laid to rest in her hometown of Bor. Her spirit and determination continue to inspire every student in the YLASS community.
