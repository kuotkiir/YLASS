Harvey Mudd College

Nyariay Kic Nyang

Independence Secondary School, Juba

Nyariay is a member of YLASS's third cohort, currently studying Computer Science at Harvey Mudd College on a full scholarship. Originating from South Sudan, a home devastated by long-term famines, heat waves, and floods, she believes specializing in CS will equip her with the right technological skills to identify trends and predict future scenarios that would subsequently help to build a climate-resilient environment, improve livelihoods, and sustain food security.

"At YLASS, I was not only exposed to the unique community that aids one stand a chance of getting into their dream college, but also a world of supportive mentors that help their students in discovering themselves and knowing their majestic potential: you could be next!!"
